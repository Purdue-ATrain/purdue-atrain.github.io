Absorbing the flood

How the lab turns a torrent of commits into a clean, compounding main.

Humans and AI agents fire commits all day across every repo. Left raw, that torrent would drown main. Instead it runs an engine: contributions pour into per-analyst branches and PRs, pass through audit gates (a regex scan, then a Claude semantic review), and the accepted flow is synthesised into the nightly basin. A human opens the final sluice — and only then does it merge into main, which rises a little richer each cycle for the next analyst to draw from.
